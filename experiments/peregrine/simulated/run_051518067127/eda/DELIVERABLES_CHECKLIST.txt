KEY FINDINGS SUMMARY
================================================================================

1. STRONG EXPONENTIAL GROWTH
   - Linear R² = 0.881
   - Log-linear R² = 0.937 (better fit)
   - Quadratic R² = 0.964 (best fit)
   - Growth rate: ~5.5% per time step

2. SEVERE OVERDISPERSION
   - Variance-to-Mean Ratio: 70.43
   - Poisson assumption VIOLATED
   - Period-specific dispersion (0.68 to 13.11)
   - No zero-inflation

3. HIGH AUTOCORRELATION
   - ACF Lag-1: 0.971
   - Durbin-Watson: 0.47
   - Residual ACF Lag-1: 0.754 (after detrending)
   - Independence assumption VIOLATED

4. REGIME SHIFTS
   - ANOVA F=151.78, p<0.001
   - Mean increases 7.8× from early to late
   - Significant changepoint at midpoint

5. NON-POISSON DISTRIBUTION
   - Chi-square GoF: p<0.001
   - Observed distribution much wider than Poisson
   - Negative Binomial or Log-Normal more appropriate

================================================================================
MODELING RECOMMENDATIONS (VERIFIED)
================================================================================

PRIMARY: Negative Binomial GLM
- Specification: C ~ NB(μ, θ), log(μ) = β₀ + β₁×year + β₂×year²
- Rationale: Handles overdispersion, count data, exponential growth
- Expected R² > 0.94

ALTERNATIVE: Log-Normal Regression  
- Specification: log(C) ~ N(μ, σ²), μ = β₀ + β₁×year + β₂×year²
- Rationale: Simple, strong fit, easy to add AR(1) errors
- Confirmed R² = 0.937

ROBUST: Quasi-Poisson with AR Errors
- Specification: C ~ Poisson(μ), Var(C)=φ×μ, AR(1) errors
- Rationale: Most robust inference for both overdispersion and correlation
- Use GEE framework
